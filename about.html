My Story

Hi, I'm Larissa, a UX designer with training in frontend development and a background in scientific research and quality control. My previous jobs showed me the importance of having a well-structured method and working as a team for a common result. Working from a lab and working as a UX designer are similar in many ways, both seek to impact lives and overcome issues that pop up in the middle of the process creatively and within a budget. I'm ready to use these same principles to create user flows and be part of a team that believes in these values. I am always looking forward to improving in this new career of choice with feedback and new knowledge. Feel free to contact me.
